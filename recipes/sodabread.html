Soda Bread Recipe

This easy Irish soda bread requires only 5 minutes of hands-on time to stir together flour, salt, sugar, baking soda, one egg, buttermilk, and butter. It emerges from the oven with the perfect crumb. No yeast required! Ready in 1-hour.
Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30-40 mins
Total Time: 40-50 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups (510 g) all-purpose flour, see notes below for making it whole grain
  • 2 teaspoons (12 g) Diamond Crystal kosher salt, see notes above
  • 1 tablespoon (13 g) sugar
  • 1 teaspoon (5 g) baking soda
  • 1 cup dried currants, optional
  • 1 egg
  • 1Â¾ cups (410 g) buttermilk, see notes above
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Step 1

Whisk together the dry ingredients.

Step 2

Add the wet ingredients: a mix of buttermilk, melted butter, and egg.

Step 3

Form into a ball using floured hands, transfer to a cast iron skillet (or other similarly sized baking vessel).

Step 4

Bake until golden.
